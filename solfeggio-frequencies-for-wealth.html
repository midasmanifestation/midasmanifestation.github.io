What Are Solfeggio Frequencies?

Solfeggio frequencies are a set of tones that date back to Gregorian chants used by medieval monks. The word "solfeggio" comes from the syllables in an old hymn: Ut (Do), Re, Mi, Fa, Sol, La. Each syllable was paired with a specific pitch. The monks believed these tones had spiritual power and used them during prayer and meditation.

The system was essentially lost for centuries until Dr. Joseph Puleo rediscovered it in the 1970s. He found patterns of six repeating frequencies hidden in the book of Numbers in the Bible. Later, Dr. Leonard Horowitz published research connecting these frequencies to specific healing effects and expanded the set to include additional tones.

Here is the thing though: there is no peer-reviewed, large-scale clinical trial that proves solfeggio frequencies do anything specific. The research that exists is mostly small studies, anecdotal reports, and theoretical frameworks based on acoustic resonance. That does not mean they do not work. It means the science is preliminary, and anyone telling you otherwise is stretching the truth.

What I can tell you from personal experience and from watching hundreds of people in online communities is that certain frequencies produce consistent effects for specific issues. The money and wealth category has a few frequencies that keep showing up in success stories. Let me walk you through the full set first, then narrow down the ones that matter for financial abundance.

I recommend listening to 396 Hz in the evening, about 30 minutes before bed. It has a calming effect that helps with sleep, and your subconscious processes information during sleep. This is not scientifically proven, but it is a consistent pattern people report.

888 Hz: The Angel Number Frequency

888 Hz is a newer addition to the solfeggio family. It does not appear in the original 9-frequency set. Some practitioners added it based on the significance of the number 8 in numerology and angel number traditions. In numerology, 8 represents abundance, financial success, and karma — the energy you put out coming back to you.

I will be honest: there is zero scientific research on 888 Hz specifically. What there is, though, is a large body of anecdotal reports from people who say this frequency produces fast, tangible results for money. The reports come from Reddit threads, YouTube comments, and manifestation forums.

The theory behind 888 Hz is that it combines the properties of 8 (material abundance) with the amplifying effect of repeating the number three times. If 528 Hz is about transformation and 396 Hz is about clearing blocks, 888 Hz is about acceleration. It is the frequency people use when they want results quickly.

I tried 888 Hz for two weeks straight. My experience: I had two unexpected financial windfalls during that period. A client paid me early. I found a $200 refund check I did not know existed. Small things, but they happened in quick succession, which felt different from random chance.

The limitation of 888 Hz is that it lacks the deeper emotional clearing work of 396 Hz or the heart-opening effect of 639 Hz. Think of it as an accelerator, not a foundation. If you have not done the inner work on your money blocks first, 888 Hz alone might not be enough.
