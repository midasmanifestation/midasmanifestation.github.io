Which Chakra Attracts Money? Activate Your Wealth Energy Centers

Most people looking for a "money chakra" are chasing the wrong idea. There is no single energy center that controls your finances. Instead, four different chakras each play a distinct role in how you earn, save, spend, and grow wealth. Here is what each one does and how to activate them.

Published June 23, 2026 14 min read

Is There a "Money Chakra"?

I get this question constantly: "Which chakra attracts money?" And the honest answer is that no single chakra controls your bank account. Anyone telling you there is one magic money chakra is oversimplifying something that touches four separate energy centers in your body.

Here is the reality. Your relationship with money is not one-dimensional. It involves feeling safe (root chakra), being creative with how you earn (sacral chakra), having the confidence to ask for what you are worth (solar plexus chakra), and believing you deserve to receive (heart chakra). Skip any one of these, and money stays stuck.

I spent two years focusing only on my root chakra, thinking security was the whole puzzle. I felt grounded, sure. But I was still undercharging for my work and avoiding opportunities because my heart chakra had a worthiness block I did not even know existed. Financial results did not shift until I addressed all four centers together.

Think of it like a car with four tires. You can inflate one perfectly, but if the other three are flat, you are not going anywhere. The chakra-for-money connection works the same way. All four need air.

The 4 Chakras That Influence Your Financial Life

Each of these four chakras handles a specific aspect of your financial life. When one is blocked, it creates a predictable pattern of money problems. When all four are open and balanced, money flows more naturally — not because of some magical law of attraction, but because you stop making decisions from fear, scarcity, or low self-worth.

Root Chakra (Muladhara) — Financial Security. This is your survival center. It governs your basic sense of safety. A blocked root chakra creates chronic money anxiety, hoarding, overspending, or total avoidance of your finances. It sits at the base of your spine and connects to your adrenal glands, which produce cortisol — the stress hormone that keeps you in fight-or-flight mode about money.

Sacral Chakra (Svadhisthana) — Creative Money Flow. This center governs creativity, pleasure, and emotional flow. When it comes to money, the sacral chakra influences how creative you are with generating income, how flexible you are with financial change, and whether you experience money as flowing freely or getting stuck. A blocked sacral chakra makes you rigid about finances and resistant to new earning methods.

Solar Plexus Chakra (Manipura) — Personal Power and Earning. This is your confidence center. It determines how much personal power you bring to your financial life. A blocked solar plexus chakra shows up as chronic undercharging, inability to negotiate, fear of asking for raises, and self-doubt about your ability to earn. It sits above your navel and connects to your willpower and determination.

Heart Chakra (Anahata) — Receiving and Worthiness. This center governs your ability to give and receive — including money. A blocked heart chakra creates a pattern where you give away your time, energy, and services for free, or you feel guilty when you earn more than others. It sits in the center of your chest and bridges the lower survival chakras with the upper spiritual ones.

Root Chakra: Your Financial Foundation

The root chakra, called Muladhara in Sanskrit, sits at the base of your spine. Its color is red. Its element is earth. Its job is to keep you alive and safe. And in modern life, money is the primary tool for survival — which is why the root chakra has such a direct connection to your finances.

When I first started studying chakras, I dismissed the root chakra as purely spiritual. But here is what changed my mind: my nervous system was constantly in survival mode, and I did not know it. I would check my bank account six times a day. I would lose sleep over bills that were already paid. I would hoard money compulsively even when I had plenty. All of this traced back to a root chakra that never felt safe.

The root chakra connects to your psoas muscle — the deepest core muscle in your body — which is directly linked to your fight-or-flight response. When the root chakra is blocked, your psoas stays chronically tight, your body stays in stress mode, and every financial decision is made from panic instead of clarity. Research from the American Psychological Association confirms that financial stress is the number one cause of anxiety in the U.S. But the stress often precedes the financial problem — your nervous system creates the anxiety first, and then your anxious decisions create the financial problems.

Childhood experiences get stored here. If you grew up hearing "money does not grow on trees" or watched your parents fight about bills, those memories imprinted on your root chakra. Your six-year-old self decided money equals danger, and that belief has been running your financial behavior ever since — without your awareness.

Signs your root chakra is blocking money:

  • Chronic money anxiety, even when your bills are paid
  • Compulsive hoarding or overspending (both come from the same root: lack of safety)
  • Avoiding your bank statements or budget entirely
  • Self-sabotage when finances improve — you unconsciously destroy progress because success does not feel safe
  • A persistent feeling that you will never have enough, regardless of your actual account balance

If three or more of these patterns sound familiar, your root chakra needs work. For a deeper dive, see our guide on root chakra and money.

Solar Plexus Chakra: Your Money Confidence Center

The solar plexus chakra, called Manipura, sits just above your navel. It is yellow. It governs your personal power, willpower, and self-esteem. When it comes to money, this chakra determines how confidently you earn, negotiate, and demand your worth.

I have talked to dozens of freelancers and business owners who know their work is valuable but cannot bring themselves to charge what they are worth. They discount their prices, offer free work, or accept low-paying contracts because asking for more triggers a visceral fear response. That fear lives in the solar plexus chakra. It is not a pricing problem. It is a power problem.

The solar plexus chakra also connects to your digestive system and your adrenal glands. When it is blocked, you might experience digestive issues, chronic fatigue, or a general feeling of being stuck — all of which show up in your financial life as procrastination, indecision, or a lack of drive to pursue higher income.

A strong solar plexus chakra does not make you greedy. It gives you the internal authority to say: "I created something valuable, and I will be compensated fairly for it." That is not arrogance. That is healthy self-worth.

Signs your solar plexus chakra is blocking money:

  • Chronic undercharging for your services or products
  • Difficulty negotiating salaries, rates, or contracts
  • Fear of asking for raises, promotions, or referrals
  • Procrastination on income-generating activities
  • A pattern of starting financial projects but never finishing them

A blocked solar plexus does not prevent you from making money. It prevents you from making enough money. For the full breakdown, read our article on solar plexus chakra and money.

Heart Chakra: Receiving and Letting Go

The heart chakra, called Anahata, sits in the center of your chest. It is green. It governs love, compassion, and — here is the part people miss — your ability to receive. Not just love. Money, opportunities, help, compliments, and abundance of all kinds.

I know a woman who gave away her consulting services for three years because she felt guilty charging people for knowledge she considered "easy." She was brilliant at what she did. Her clients made tens of thousands from her advice. But she could not bring herself to invoice them properly because her heart chakra was blocked. She equated receiving money with being selfish.

The heart chakra also governs your relationship with debt. People with blocked heart chakras tend to carry guilt about past financial mistakes long after those mistakes should have been released. They punish themselves by refusing to earn more. They hold onto debt as a form of penance. The heart chakra needs to learn that letting go of financial shame is not the same as being irresponsible.

When the heart chakra opens, something shifts. You stop giving away your value for free. You stop feeling bad about earning. You start to see receiving money as a natural exchange — not a moral failing. The shift is subtle, but its financial impact is enormous.

Signs your heart chakra is blocking money:

  • Giving away your time, energy, and expertise for free
  • Guilt or shame when you earn more than others
  • Difficulty receiving compliments, help, or financial support
  • Holding onto past financial mistakes or debt guilt
  • A pattern of overworking for minimal financial return because you do not feel you deserve more

The heart chakra bridges your survival chakras (root and sacral) with your spiritual chakras (throat, third eye, and crown). When it is blocked, the energy from the lower chakras cannot reach the higher ones, and vice versa. It is the gateway — which is why clearing it creates such a dramatic financial shift.

Sacral Chakra: Creative Money Flow

The sacral chakra, called Svadhisthana, sits just below your navel. It is orange. It governs creativity, pleasure, emotions, and flow. In the context of money, the sacral chakra determines how creative you are with income generation and how freely money moves through your life.

I have noticed a pattern among people with blocked sacral chakras: they are rigid about money. They stick to one income source, resist trying new financial strategies, and feel threatened by any change to their financial routine. The sacral chakra is about fluidity and adaptability. When it is blocked, money stagnates — not because opportunities are absent, but because you cannot flow with them.

A blocked sacral chakra also affects your emotional relationship with spending. You either hoard money out of fear (scarcity mindset) or spend compulsively to fill an emotional void (numbing mindset). Both patterns come from the same root: a sacral chakra that cannot feel pleasure in balanced, healthy ways.

When the sacral chakra is open, money becomes more fluid. You experiment with side income. You adapt when your financial situation changes. You find creative solutions to money problems instead of panicking. You also experience less guilt about enjoying the money you earn — which, paradoxically, makes you more motivated to earn more.

Signs your sacral chakra is blocking money:

  • Rigid adherence to one income source with no interest in alternatives
  • Compulsive spending driven by emotion rather than need
  • Guilt about spending money on yourself, even when you can afford it
  • Creative blocks that prevent you from innovating in your business or career
  • A pattern of money flowing in and immediately flowing back out

The sacral chakra is often overlooked in money conversations because people focus on security (root) and power (solar plexus). But without creative flow, those two chakras can only take you so far. You need the sacral energy to keep money moving and growing.

Here is what I have tested personally and seen work consistently across different people. Each chakra responds to specific techniques. Pick one or two methods per chakra and practice daily. Consistency matters more than intensity.

2. Root chakra affirmations. Say these while standing with feet flat on the floor: "I am safe. I am supported. I have enough." "Money flows to me because I am worthy of security." The physical posture matters — it signals safety to your nervous system.

4. Malasana yoga pose. Deep squat that opens the pelvic floor and activates the root chakra. Hold for 30 seconds to 1 minute, 3 times per week.

Solar Plexus Activation Methods

1. Power posing. Stand in a wide stance with hands on hips for 2 minutes before financial conversations — negotiations, sales calls, or salary discussions. This activates the solar plexus and increases confidence.

2. Solar plexus affirmations. "I am powerful. I earn what I deserve." "I create value and I am compensated for it." Say these while pressing your hand gently against your upper abdomen.

3. 528 Hz frequency meditation. The 528 Hz frequency — the "love frequency" — strengthens personal power and self-worth. Listen for 20 minutes daily.

4. Boat pose (Navasana). This yoga pose directly activates the solar plexus area. Hold for 15 to 30 seconds, 3 sets, 3 times per week.

Heart Chakra Activation Methods

1. Heart-opening stretches. Cobra pose and camel pose open the chest area and release stored emotional tension. Practice for 5 to 10 minutes daily.

2. Heart chakra affirmations. "I deserve to receive abundance." "I release guilt about earning." "Money is an expression of value, and I have value." Write these down and read them aloud each morning.

3. Gratitude practice. Write down 5 things you are grateful for each evening. Gratitude shifts your heart chakra from "I do not have enough" to "I already have abundance." This is not woo-woo — gratitude rewires neural pathways associated with receiving.

4. 639 Hz frequency meditation. The 639 Hz frequency targets the heart chakra and strengthens your ability to give and receive. Listen for 15 to 20 minutes daily.

Sacral Chakra Activation Methods

1. Creative expression. Paint, dance, write, cook, or do anything that activates your creative flow. The sacral chakra does not distinguish between creative outlets — any creativity strengthens it.

2. Water immersion. The sacral chakra is connected to the water element. Take warm baths, swim, or even sit near flowing water. Spending 20 minutes in warm water activates the sacral chakra naturally.

3. Sacral chakra affirmations. "Money flows to me easily and often." "I am creative and I find new ways to earn." "I enjoy spending money on things that matter to me." The last one is important — it breaks the guilt pattern.

4. 417 Hz frequency meditation. The 417 Hz frequency facilitates change and clears energy blockages in the sacral chakra. Listen for 15 minutes daily, ideally in the morning.

The root chakra is the most foundational for wealth because it governs your sense of safety and security — the bedrock of financial stability.
