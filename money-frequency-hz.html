Money Frequency Hz: Which Sound Frequencies Attract Financial Abundance

Published: June 23, 2026 | Reading time: 12 min

Not all money frequencies work the same way. Here is a straight comparison of the top Hz tones for wealth so you can pick the right one instead of guessing.

TL;DR

Money frequencies are specific sound tones measured in Hertz (Hz) used in sound therapy to shift your mental state toward wealth attraction. The most popular are 369 Hz (root chakra, financial security), 528 Hz (transformation, abundance), 888 Hz (prosperity, abundance multiplier), 432 Hz (harmony, relaxation), and 963 Hz (divine connection, higher purpose). For money manifestation specifically, 369 Hz and 528 Hz are the most commonly recommended starting points.

What Is a “Money Frequency”?

I spent weeks going down this rabbit hole before I started writing about it. The phrase “money frequency” gets thrown around like everyone agrees on what it means, but nobody actually defines it clearly. So here is my take after reading dozens of studies, forums, and practitioner reports.

A money frequency is a specific sound tone, measured in Hertz (Hz), that practitioners believe can shift your mental and emotional state toward wealth attraction. The idea is rooted in the concept that everything vibrates at a certain rate -- your body, your thoughts, your bank account. By listening to a frequency that matches the vibration of abundance, you supposedly align yourself with financial opportunities.

Where does this idea come from? Two main sources. First, the solfeggio frequencies, a set of tones originally derived from medieval Gregorian chants. Dr. Joseph Puleo popularized the modern 6-tone system in the 1970s, claiming each tone carries specific healing properties. Second, the broader field of brainwave entrainment, which is actual science -- sound frequencies can influence brainwave patterns. Binaural beats, isochronic tones, and monaural beats have measurable effects on mental states like relaxation, focus, and sleep.

Here is what I want to be honest about from the start: there is no peer-reviewed study proving that listening to 369 Hz or 528 Hz will make money appear in your bank account. What the research does support is that certain frequencies can reduce anxiety, improve focus, and shift you into a mental state where you make better financial decisions. That alone makes the practice worth trying -- just do not expect magic without action.

The term “money frequency Hz” has picked up serious search volume because people are looking for a shortcut. I get it. But the real value is not in one magic number. It is in finding the frequency that addresses your specific block to wealth and using it consistently. That is what the rest of this guide covers.

369 Hz: The Root Chakra Wealth Frequency

369 Hz sits at the heart of the money frequency conversation, and for good reason. It targets the root chakra -- the energy center at the base of your spine that governs your sense of safety, survival, and material security. If you have ever felt anxious checking your bank balance, avoided opening bills, or froze up when someone asked your rate, your root chakra might be where the block lives.

The connection to Nikola Tesla is what makes 369 Hz famous. Tesla was obsessed with the numbers 3, 6, and 9. He reportedly said, “If you only knew the magnificence of the 3, 6, and 9, then you would have the key to the universe.” Whether Tesla actually said that is debatable -- it gets attributed to him a lot on the internet -- but the numerological pattern is real in the sense that these numbers recur throughout mathematics and physics.

In solfeggio theory, 369 Hz is the “frequency of miracles” connected to removing energetic blocks. Practitioners report that listening to 369 Hz regularly helps with: reduced financial anxiety, increased confidence in money conversations, noticing opportunities they previously overlooked, and a general sense of calm around topics that used to trigger panic.

528 Hz: The Miracle Abundance Frequency

528 Hz might be the most popular solfeggio frequency overall -- not just for money, but for everything. It is called the “love frequency” and the “miracle tone” because practitioners believe it carries a vibration of transformation at the cellular level. DNA repair studies sometimes get cited here, though those studies were small and the connection to attracting money is a stretch.

What 528 Hz actually does well is shift your emotional state. If you are stuck in a pattern of scarcity thinking -- always worried there is not enough, always expecting financial disappointment -- 528 Hz works on the emotional root of that pattern. It targets the solar plexus and heart chakras, which deal with personal power and emotional openness.

For money specifically, 528 Hz is strongest when your problem is not fear (that is 369 Hz territory) but rather rigidity. When you are so locked into your current financial situation that you cannot see exits, opportunities, or creative solutions. The frequency is associated with breaking old patterns and welcoming new possibilities.

888 Hz: The Angel Number for Prosperity

888 Hz is not part of the traditional solfeggio scale -- it is a newer addition that comes from the angel number tradition rather than ancient numerology. In angel number interpretation, seeing 888 is a signal that financial abundance is heading your way. The frequency 888 Hz attempts to capture that vibration in sound form.

The concept here is prosperity consciousness -- the idea that wealth is not something you chase but something you tune into. If your core issue is that deep-down you believe you do not deserve money, or that rich people are inherently different from you, 888 Hz is designed to address that belief system.

It works primarily on the crown and third eye chakras, which relate to spiritual connection and intuition. The theory is that by opening these upper energy centers, you align yourself with universal abundance rather than operating from a place of scarcity and effort.

In practice, 888 Hz tends to produce a meditative, expansive state. People report feeling connected to something larger during sessions, which might sound vague but actually matters for wealth creation. When you feel connected and abundant rather than desperate and grasping, you make fundamentally different financial decisions.

432 Hz: The Universal Harmony Frequency

432 Hz occupies an interesting space in the frequency world. It is not specifically a money frequency the way 369 Hz is. Instead, 432 Hz is associated with universal harmony -- the idea that this specific tuning creates a vibration that resonates with natural patterns found throughout the universe, from the orbit of planets to the spiral of a seashell.

Some musicians and audio engineers swear that 432 Hz sounds warmer and more relaxed than the standard 440 Hz tuning used in modern music. Whether that is psychoacoustic bias or real measurable difference is debated. What is not debated is that 432 Hz music tends to induce a calmer, more centered state in listeners.

For money manifestation, 432 Hz works best when financial anxiety is your primary obstacle. If money stress keeps you up at night, if you feel a constant low-level dread about your finances, or if financial decisions always feel overwhelming, 432 Hz helps restore balance. It does not target wealth directly -- it removes the noise that blocks your ability to think clearly about money.

I would not start with 432 Hz if your main goal is attracting a specific financial outcome. Start with 369 Hz or 528 Hz instead. But if you have tried everything else and your problem is that you cannot stay calm enough to follow through, 432 Hz might be the missing piece.

963 Hz: Higher Consciousness and Abundance

963 Hz sits at the top of the solfeggio scale and connects to the crown chakra -- the energy center associated with spiritual connection, higher wisdom, and transcendence. Of all the frequencies on this list, 963 Hz is the least directly connected to money and the most connected to purpose.

Here is why that matters for wealth: some of the most financially successful people I have studied attribute their wealth to finding the intersection between what they love doing and what the market values. 963 Hz is not about attracting dollars. It is about connecting with the deeper sense of purpose that drives sustainable wealth creation.

People who work with 963 Hz report experiences like: clearer intuition about which opportunities to pursue, a sense of being guided toward the right people and projects, reduced attachment to outcomes (which paradoxically improves results), and a feeling that money flows when you are aligned rather than when you hustle.

I will be blunt: if you need to pay rent next week, 963 Hz is not your solution. This is a longer-game frequency. It works on the version of you that makes decisions 6 and 12 months from now. Combine it with 369 Hz for a balanced approach that addresses both immediate material needs and long-term alignment.

Jupiter Frequencies (183.58 Hz, 473.9 Hz): Planetary Prosperity

Jupiter frequencies are the wild card on this list. They are not solfeggio tones -- they come from the practice of planetary attunement, where specific Hz values are derived from the orbital period and electromagnetic properties of planets. Jupiter, the planet of expansion, growth, and abundance, has two frequencies that practitioners associate with prosperity.

183.58 Hz is Jupiter’s base frequency. Think of it as the foundational vibration of expansion. People who work with this frequency report feeling more open to growth, more willing to take calculated risks, and more comfortable with the idea that their income can be larger than their current reality suggests. It is a grounding, stabilizing energy that says growth is natural and safe.

473.9 Hz is a Jupiter harmonic -- a higher-pitched, more active version of the same planetary energy. Where 183.58 Hz is about being open to growth, 473.9 Hz is about actively attracting opportunities. Entrepreneurs and investors tend to gravitate toward this frequency because it aligns with the mindset of seeking and creating new ventures.

The honest limitation here: planetary frequencies have even less scientific backing than solfeggio tones. The entire system is based on the idea that planets emit electromagnetic frequencies that interact with human energy fields. That is a metaphysical claim, not a measurable one. But enough practitioners report positive results that I think these frequencies deserve a mention, especially for people who have tried solfeggio frequencies without the response they wanted.

How to Choose the Right Frequency for You

After writing about each frequency individually, I realized the most useful thing I can do is help you pick one instead of trying all seven at once. Here is the decision framework I use:

1

Your Problem Is Fear About Money

You avoid checking your bank account. You feel anxious when bills arrive. You undercharge because asking for more money makes you uncomfortable. Start with 369 Hz. This is the most direct frequency for root chakra money blocks.

2

Your Problem Is Stagnation

You are comfortable but stuck. Same income for years. Same financial patterns repeating. You know you need to change but cannot seem to break the cycle. Start with 528 Hz. It targets transformation and pattern-breaking.

3

Your Problem Is Scarcity Mindset

You believe money is hard to earn, that there is never enough, or that wealthy people play by different rules. You need to shift your fundamental beliefs about abundance. Start with 888 Hz. It works on prosperity consciousness at the belief level.

4

Your Problem Is Financial Anxiety

You can think about money rationally but cannot stay calm long enough to act on what you know. Anxiety makes you reactive, impulsive, or paralyzed. Start with 432 Hz. It restores harmony and calm.

5

Your Problem Is Lack of Direction

You do not know what you want. You are not sure what kind of wealth you are building or why. Decisions feel unclear. Start with 963 Hz. It connects you with purpose and intuition.

6

Your Problem Is Growth Limitation

You have hit a ceiling. Your business or career is not expanding the way you want. You need to break through to the next level. Try Jupiter frequencies (183.58 Hz or 473.9 Hz). They target expansion energy directly.

The shortcut: If you are not sure where to start, begin with 369 Hz for 21 days. If you notice a shift in how you feel about money, continue. If not, switch to 528 Hz for the next 21 days. Most people find their frequency within three rounds of this process. Do not try to listen to all seven frequencies at once -- that is like going to the gym and trying every machine in the first workout. Pick one. Commit. Evaluate. Adjust.
