The Traditional 7 Chakras

Most people stop at seven. The root chakra at the base of your spine, the sacral below your navel, the solar plexus at your gut, the heart in the center of your chest, the throat, the third eye between your eyebrows, and the crown at the top of your head. That is the system you will find in most yoga studios, most books, and most online articles.

Each of these seven energy centers governs specific areas of your life. Your root chakra controls survival, safety, and your relationship with money. Your sacral handles creativity, pleasure, and emotional flow. The solar plexus is your personal power center — confidence, willpower, and self-worth live here. Your heart chakra manages love, compassion, and your ability to give and receive. The throat governs communication and truth. Your third eye handles intuition and inner knowing. And the crown connects you to something bigger than yourself — purpose, spirituality, and divine guidance.

The seven-chakra system works. I have used it. It helped me understand why I kept repeating the same financial patterns, why I could never save money no matter how much I earned, and why I always felt like something was blocking my progress. When I focused on my root chakra money blocks and my solar plexus self-worth issues, things shifted. Slowly, but they shifted.

But here is the problem: the seven-chakra system only covers the energy body from your feet to the top of your head. It does not address the chakras that exist below the feet or above the crown — and those are exactly the ones that control your access to universal abundance. That is where the 12 chakras manifestation system comes in.
